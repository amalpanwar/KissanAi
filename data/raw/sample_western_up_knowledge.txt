Western Uttar Pradesh agriculture baseline knowledge:
- Major crops: Wheat, Sugarcane, Potato, Mustard, Paddy, Maize.
- Irrigated belts often support wheat-potato rotations.
- Sugarcane is cash positive but ties up land for long duration.
- Potato can be high return with high input cost and storage risk.
- Wheat has moderate cost and relatively stable demand.
- Mustard suits lower irrigation situations in Rabi.
- Districts such as Meerut, Muzaffarnagar, Baghpat have strong sugarcane ecosystems.
- Western UP farmers should evaluate MSP-linked crops vs open market crops based on risk appetite.
